Community Guidelines

At ConnectHub, we believe in fostering a safe, respectful, and inclusive community where everyone can connect, share, and engage with others. These guidelines are designed to help create a positive experience for all users. By using ConnectHub, you agree to follow these guidelines and our Terms of Service.

Be Respectful

Treat others with respect and kindness, even when discussing controversial topics or disagreeing with someone's opinion. Remember that behind every username is a real person.

  • Do not engage in personal attacks, harassment, or bullying
  • Avoid inflammatory or disrespectful language
  • Listen to other perspectives and engage in constructive dialogue

Protect Privacy

Respect the privacy of others and yourself. Sharing personal information without consent is prohibited.

  • Do not share personal information about others
  • Be cautious about sharing your own personal information
  • Do not attempt to identify anonymous users

Prohibited Content

The following types of content are prohibited on ConnectHub:

  • Hate speech, discrimination, or content that promotes intolerance
  • Violent, graphic, or shocking content
  • Adult or sexually explicit content
  • Content advocating for illegal activities
  • Spam, scams, or misleading information
  • Content that violates intellectual property rights
  • Personally identifiable information without consent
  • Malware, phishing attempts, or harmful code

No Harassment

We have zero tolerance for harassment in any form. This includes:

  • Targeted insults or attacks
  • Unwanted sexual attention or advances
  • Intimidation or threats
  • Encouraging others to harass someone
  • Stalking or persistent unwanted contact

Authenticity

We value authentic interactions and accurate information:

  • Do not impersonate others or create misleading accounts
  • Do not spread misinformation or fake news
  • Do not use bots or automated systems to engage with content
  • Do not artificially inflate engagement (likes, shares, etc.)

Community Moderation

Our community is moderated by both automated systems and human moderators:

  • Report content that violates our guidelines
  • Respect moderator decisions and instructions
  • Do not attempt to circumvent moderation actions
  • Contact us if you believe a moderation decision was made in error

Reporting Violations

If you see content that violates these guidelines, please report it:

  • Use the report button on posts, comments, or profiles
  • Provide specific details about the violation
  • Don't engage with content that violates guidelines

Consequences

Violating these guidelines may result in one or more of the following actions:

  • Content removal
  • Warnings
  • Temporary restrictions or account limitations
  • Permanent suspension of account
  • In serious cases, legal action may be taken

Updates to Guidelines

These guidelines may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes, but we encourage you to review them periodically.

Questions?

If you have questions about these guidelines or need to report something that doesn't have a report option, please contact us.
